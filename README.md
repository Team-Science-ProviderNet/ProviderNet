# ProviderNet
ProviderNet exists to eliminate the barriers of geography in healthcare so no information gap, insurance blindspot, or clinical oversight harms patient outcomes. 
We unify health records and insurance coverage, deliver easy to read summaries using AI powered tools to allow patients to quickly and efficiently understand test results, and guide patients to providers covered in their network. 
ProviderNet is the only platform that unifies the healthcare journey into a single workflow. 
We’ve replaced the digital chaos of multiple portals with one simple login, giving patients and providers a guided care experience that transcends state lines. 
ProviderNet is the missing piece to the puzzle of truly connected, patient-centered healthcare.
